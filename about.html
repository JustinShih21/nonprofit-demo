About Us

Without Limits Foundation began as a student-led club at Millikan High School in Long Beach, CA. What started as after-school dance classes for the school's diverse disabled community soon transformed the school culture—igniting joy, daily high fives, and deeper student connections.

Today, Without Limits is a staple in the Long Beach community, creating welcoming spaces where neurodiverse youth and adults celebrate their unique abilities through dance and sports. At the heart of our program are one-on-one buddy pairings with neurotypical peers who gain empathy, leadership, and advocacy skills.

What Makes Us Unique

Our one-on-one buddy system pairs each neurodiverse participant with a neurotypical volunteer. This creates powerful bonds that support physical development and deep emotional and social growth.
